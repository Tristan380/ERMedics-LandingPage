courses

Basic Accident and Emergency Care Course

Learn essential skills to handle accidents and emergency situations effectively. Gain knowledge in assessing scenes, providing first aid, managing injuries, and delivering emergency care.

Firefighting Courses

Develop skills to respond to fires and emergencies involving fire hazards. Learn fire prevention, safety protocols, proper use of extinguishers, and basic firefighting techniques.

Online Courses

We offer online courses that teach you emergency care skills, including CPR, AED use, and BLS for adults, children, and infants. The courses are designed to be convenient and affordable, so you can learn at your own pace.

AHA BLS Courses

Master life-saving interventions for cardiac emergencies. Acquire skills in CPR, using AEDs, and providing basic life support for adults, children, and infants.
